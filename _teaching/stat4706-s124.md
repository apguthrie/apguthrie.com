Undergraduate introductory statistics course covering sampling distributions, estimation, hypothesis testing, simple linear regression, multiple regression, and one-way analysis of variance with applications in engineering. Six-week online asynchronous course, 25 students. **Taught in Summer 2024.**

**Course Objectives:**
Students will learn to apply basic concepts of probability and statistical analysis with a focus on statistical thinking. Students will be able to perform the analyses on the latest statistical software (R or Python).

**Textbook:** *Probability & Statistics for Engineers and Scientists*, 9th Edition, by Walpole, Myers, Myers, and Ye.
